Hi, I'm Toye. I am an engineer focused on digital forensics, cybersecurity, and cloud systems.

My work involves figuring out how systems work—and how they break. I started in IT support and now spend my time building serverless solutions, exploring AI tools, and researching digital forensic techniques.

I have a background in electrical engineering, and I like connecting different areas of technology.

I am currently studying for my Master of Science in Cyber Forensics. I build mostly with Python, Go, and AWS. When I am not writing code or investigating a system, I am usually reading about space exploration or planning my next project.

This blog is my personal lab. It is where I document my projects, share the things I learn, and test new ideas. I hope you find something useful here. Take a look around!
